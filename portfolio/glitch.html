I used Photoshop and Audacity to create Glitch for first Glitch video and second glitch image of Mondrian. It is a picture of myself and I also added sound to sync the speed of image changes in the glitch video.

Theme of the glitch project for fourth and third image is happy and guilty relationship with fast-food. We all know that pizza and hamburgers are delicious. They are rich in protein but also have cholesterol and saturated fat. For fourth image, there are 56mg cholesterol per hamburger so I divided the image like half original and half with glitch.

I thought It would be challenging to find where is the half of the image in Text editor but It was so much easier with Audacity. I like it because I didn’t have to worry about first 200 words rules which cause an error since I am using Audacity for editing. But also it was hard to find the middle of the image in text editor. I think it turned out less appetizing but great. For the last image, there are 7g of saturated fat in 100g of pizza.

So I tried to make at least 7 different glitch effects using Audacity. Fade in, Fade out, Repeat, Change Tempo, Reverb, Reverse and Distortion. I especially liked Reverb because it gives a smooth glitch dots over the image like a glitch clouds. Also, I add small glitches on the pizza like an additional toppings. Overall, It was a cool project because now I know one new program for image editing.
